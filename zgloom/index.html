Modern ports of the Amiga FPS Gloom

4 Games · 1 Launcher Windows · macOS · Android · Vita Amiga FPS Reborn

ZGloom Collection keeps Black Magic’s wonderfully messy Amiga shooter spirit alive on modern hardware: launch Gloom, Gloom Deluxe, Gloom 3 and Zombie Massacre with cleaner rendering, widescreen options, improved lighting, optional atmosphere effects and ports tuned for desktop, TV and handheld play.

Highlights

  • Modern source-port collection for multiple Gloom-engine games
  • Built-in launcher for Gloom, Gloom Deluxe, Gloom 3 and Zombie Massacre
  • 4:3 and 16:9 display modes with adjustable field of view
  • Improved renderer, lighting, muzzle flashes, reflections and dust particles

Requirements

  • Supported device for the selected port: Windows, macOS, Android/OUYA or PS Vita
  • Original or freely released compatible Gloom data files
  • SDL2 / Vita2D runtime dependencies depending on platform build
  • Gamepad recommended for TV and handheld versions
