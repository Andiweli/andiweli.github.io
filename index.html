ZGloom – Modern Ports of the Amiga FPS Gloom

Play Gloom, Gloom Deluxe, Gloom 3 and Zombie Massacre today on Windows, macOS, Android/OUYA and PlayStation Vita with enhanced rendering, widescreen support and extended quality-of-life features.

What is Gloom?

Gloom is a 1995 Amiga first-person shooter by Black Magic Software, known for its fast action, gory combat and heavy hardware demands at the time. Later expanded into Gloom Deluxe / Ultimate Gloom, Gloom 3 and Zombie Massacre, the game has become a cult classic. ZGloom is a modern multi-platform re-implementation of the engine.

ZGloom Features

  • SDL2-based modern C++ engine for current hardware.
  • Supports Gloom, Gloom Deluxe, Gloom 3 and Zombie Massacre.
  • Improved renderer with fixed perspective, muzzle flash, lights and polish.
  • Optional post-processing: vignette, film-grain, scanlines CRT look.
  • 4:3 or 16:9 widescreen modes, adjustable field of view.
  • Multi-game launcher, in-game settings, Save/Load features.
  • Gamepad-friendly controls on console and mobile platforms.
